During last 20 years, China has experienced a huge change, and achieved an effective role in world market. Chinese products are used to be known as cheap and low quality products, but recently China government has focused on increasing the quality and changing the Chinese brands from” low quality, low price” to “high quality, reasonable price”. Considering this fact, our company is trying to find and introduce the most reliable Chinese companies to its customers. After more than 10 years cooperation with Chinese manufacturers and suppliers, we decided to establish our new company -Gemini Global - in Beijing in 2010. Main business of the company is importing raw material to China and exporting production lines and different types of machinery and other products from china. Gemini Global's main market is IRAN and some other Middle East countries.
