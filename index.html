Gemini Global Trading co. ltd. is an established privately held company with offices in China and Iran, providing trading and investment services.

The company started trading activity between Iran and China in 2000, and established its Beijing office in 2009 under the name Gemini Global Trading, mainly trading Iranian Carpets, Handicraft, and other cultural products.

Gemini Global Trading co. ltd. is a member of Iranian Chamber of Commerce in China since 2011.
